System Architect

Project: Overall Solution for Soft Foundation Treatment

As the main technical leader, I participated in all aspects of the project, including PoC, field experiment, research, development, test, and product transformation, installation and deployment on-site, upgrade and function expansion, etc. This product has been expanded to support more than 6 different types of construction equipment.

After a long-term field investigation and customer visit, a pile construction management system suitable for the national conditions is developed according to the customer demand. In the construction process of a piling machine, it will automatically and continuously monitor, analyze and record the pile forming parameters, that are required by national and industrial standards for quality assessment. And this is a very significant transfer, because the system not only acquires the data, such as location, depth, pile start time and end time, the sink-lift times, etc., originally collected by field operator through observation, but also many very important parameters that could no easily be collected before, such as instant current, force bearing layer current, filling coefficient, sink speed, fed gravel or concrete volume, etc. Through the acquisition, intelligent analysis, and processing of a variety of sensor data, the key parameters and the real-time data are uploaded to the management platform for further mining, statistical analysis for reporting; And this makes it possible for the engineering manager to control the quality, progress, and productivity, and even make a quick decision. Besides, the system's high-precision navigation ability could navigate the machine to the piling point quickly, without pre-stakeout, and improve the productivity of each machine dramatically.

  • System Components: Controller and Sensors (Embedded, C）, On-site Terminal（Win7, C#, WPF）, Data Communication, Aggregation and Analyze（C#, MySQL, Message Queue）, Web Platform（Bootstrap, Node.js, MySQL）
  • Time Sync: The internal clock of the system is calibrated through GPS time, and ensure the time synchronization of multiple sensors and various system modules
  • Sensors: Dual-Ant-GNSS with INS for RTK position（Horizontal<1cm, Vertical<2cm）and attitude（Pitch/Roll<0.1deg, Yaw<0.09deg）, Current Sensor, Weight Sensor, Laser for distance, Switch sensor for 0-1 signal, Rotary Encoder, Tilt Sensor, Flowmeter, Hydraulic sensor
  • Bus: CAN, RS485
